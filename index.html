Biography

Amila Bacchus

New York City born and raised artist. I attend Hunter College, majoring in Emerging Media and minoring in Women Gender Studies. Throughout my studies, I have grown my skills in Adobe Photoshop, Premiere, and Illustrator.

Artist Statement

I create art and designs in pursuit of helping young girls of color explore the arts and everything this field has to offer. Many times we are pushed to attain careers in the Sciences and neglect creative opportunities, but the arts have just as much merit and value that can help young girls to lead more fulfilling lives!
